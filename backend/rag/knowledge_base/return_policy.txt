DRUTA KART — RETURN POLICY

Due to food safety regulations, most items are NOT eligible for physical return. We issue refunds or replacements instead.

## Items Eligible for Replacement
- Wrong item delivered
- Damaged packaging (product intact but packaging broken)
- Missing items from a multi-item order
- Expired or near-expired perishables

## Items Requiring Photo Evidence
- Visibly damaged products
- Expired items
- Contaminated food items

## Replacement Process
1. Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery with a clear photo
2. Support team reviews within 30 minutes
3. If approved, replacement dispatched from nearest dark store
4. Expected delivery: same day (reported before 8 PM) or next day

## Items Not Eligible for Return or Replacement
- Products consumed or partially used (unless proven defective before use)
- Perishable items reported more than 24 hours after delivery
- Items damaged during customer handling after delivery
- Promotional/free items included with an order

## Dispute Resolution
- Request human review — supervisor contacts you within 2 hours
- Email grievances@drutakart.in with order ID and photos
