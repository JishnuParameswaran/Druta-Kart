DRUTA KART — REFUND POLICY

## Eligibility for Refunds
You are entitled to a full refund if:
- Your order was not delivered within 60 minutes of the promised delivery time
- You received a damaged, expired, or contaminated product
- You received the wrong product(s)
- Your order was cancelled by Druta Kart due to stock unavailability
- A payment was charged but the order was not confirmed

## Refund Methods
1. Original payment method: Credited within 5–7 business days
2. Druta Kart Wallet: Up to ₹200 per transaction, applied instantly

## Refund Timeline
- Wallet credit: Instant (within minutes)
- UPI / net banking: 1–3 business days
- Credit / debit card: 5–7 business days

## Non-Refundable Items
- Perishable items accepted without noting damage at delivery
- Items damaged after delivery due to customer handling
- Orders placed by mistake after delivery has been completed

## How to Request a Refund
1. Contact support within 24 hours of delivery
2. Provide your order ID and a photo of the issue (for damaged/wrong items)
3. Our team will verify and initiate the refund within 2 hours of approval

## Partial Refunds
If only part of your order is affected, you will receive a partial refund for the affected items only.
